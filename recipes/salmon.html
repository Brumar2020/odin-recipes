Garlic Butter Salmon Recipe

Description

Simple, but tasty salmon dish basted in butter then cooked in a pan with garlic.

Ingredients

Amount varies depending on the amount you are seving for.

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp (~2 cloves) olive oil
  • 6 Tbsp salted butter (cut into cubes)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Season salmon with salt and pepper
  2. Heat oil in large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Sear curved side of salmon and cook for 3 minutes until golden.
  4. Butter and garlic - Turn the salmon, cook for another minute, then add butter. When the butter melts, add garlic.
  5. Baste the salmon for 1 minute and 30 seconds after adding the garlic and before it starts turning golden. In order to baste, tilt the pan slightly so the butter pools on one side, then scoop up the butter using a large spoon and spoon it over the salmon.
  6. Rest for 3 minutes then serve with garlic butter and lemon juice.

ENJOY!!!
