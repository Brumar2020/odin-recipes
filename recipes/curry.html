Curry Chicken Recipe

Description

Indian-inspired chicken curry features chicken stewed in a tomatp-based sauce with flavorful aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 chopped (small) onion
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 3 Tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp ground cinammon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp grated fresh ginger root
  • 1/2 tsp white sugar
  • Some salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts cut into small pieces
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat. PLace onions and sauté until light brown.
  2. Add and stir garlic, curry powder, cinammon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar and salt. Stir for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, then simmer fo 20-25 minutes.
  4. Remove bay leaf, add lemon juice and cayenne pepper and stir until mixed. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Bon Appétit!!!
